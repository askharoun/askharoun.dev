Karaoke Mixer PCB

A comprehensive five-stage audio-processing circuit designed for karaoke applications, featuring vocal suppression and advanced tone control capabilities.

Category School Project
Duration 4 Months
Team Size Individual
Status Completed

Project Overview

This project involved designing and implementing a sophisticated karaoke mixer circuit that processes audio signals through five distinct stages. The system incorporates advanced analog circuit design principles to achieve high-quality audio processing with minimal distortion.

The mixer features vocal suppression capabilities, allowing users to remove or reduce vocal tracks from stereo recordings, making it ideal for karaoke applications. Additionally, the circuit includes comprehensive tone control functionality for fine-tuning audio output.

Key Features

Vocal Suppression

Advanced algorithm to isolate and suppress vocal frequencies while preserving instrumental tracks.

5-Stage Processing

Multi-stage audio processing pipeline for optimal signal conditioning and enhancement.

Tone Control

Comprehensive bass, mid, and treble controls for precise audio customization.

Low Distortion

Optimized circuit design achieving less than 0.1% total harmonic distortion.

Technical Implementation

The circuit design utilizes high-quality operational amplifiers in various configurations including inverting, non-inverting, and differential amplifiers. Each stage is carefully designed to maintain signal integrity while providing the necessary processing capabilities.
