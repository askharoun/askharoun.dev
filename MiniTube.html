MiniTube – Persistent YouTube Mini Player

A Chrome extension that keeps your YouTube video in a floating mini player while you browse. Built for lectures, tutorials, and background videos.

Available on the Chrome Web Store
Open MiniTube on Chrome Web Store
Click to install directly in Chrome

What MiniTube does

MiniTube fixes one annoying thing about YouTube: losing your video the second you click around. With MiniTube on, your current video lives in a small floating player so you can scroll, read comments, or open new pages without starting over.

  • Floating mini player that stays on top of YouTube pages.
  • No restart when you navigate between videos or sections.
  • Simple size options instead of glitchy drag-resize behavior.
  • Designed for long-form content like lectures and tutorials.

Tech and my role

I built MiniTube as a solo project to practice real-world browser extension development and UX thinking.

Tech stack Manifest V3, JavaScript, HTML, CSS
APIs chrome.runtime, chrome.tabs, content scripts
Main use case Watching EE lectures, coding videos, and tutorials while multitasking
What I learned Single-page app navigation, extension structure, floating UI design
