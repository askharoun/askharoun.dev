SEO & UTILITY FAQ

How do I save a clipboard image as PNG?

Simply open PixelPaste, press Ctrl+V, type a name, and hit Enter. It's the fastest clipboard to file converter available.

Is my data secure?

PixelPaste is a 100% private offline tool. Your images are never uploaded to a server; they stay in your browser memory.
