Email Code Extractor

Automatically detect and copy OTP/2FA codes directly from your Gmail inbox list. Stop opening emails just to get a six-digit code.

Available on the Chrome Web Store
Get Email Code Extractor
Add to Chrome (Free)

What it does

Logging into websites usually involves a "verify your email" step. This disrupts your flow by forcing you to open the email, find the code, copy it, and go back.

Email Code Extractor solves this by scanning your inbox rows for patterns like "Code: 123456" and giving you a button to copy it instantly.

  • Detects numeric verification codes in subject lines/snippets.
  • Adds a "Copy Code" button directly to the Gmail inbox row.
  • Copies to clipboard instantly with one click.
  • Privacy First: Runs entirely locally in RAM. No data is stored or sent.
