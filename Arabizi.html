What is Arabizi / Franco Arabic?

Arabizi, also called Franco Arabic, is Arabic written with Latin letters and numbers. Instead of typing the Arabic alphabet, people use English letters and digits to represent sounds:

  • 3 is used for ع
  • 7 is used for ح
  • 5 or 7' can represent خ
  • 2 can represent a glottal stop ء

It’s common in WhatsApp chats, Instagram DMs, Messenger, gaming chats, and more. A lot of people search for phrases like “Arabizi to English”, “Franco Arabic translator”, “Franco to English”, or even specific phrases like “a3mel eh meaning” or “mesh fahim 7aga meaning”.

This online Arabizi translator takes your Franco Arabic text, converts it to Arabic script, and then to natural English so you can quickly understand what a sentence means.

What is Arabizi?

Arabizi, or Franco Arabic, is Arabic written using the English alphabet plus numbers like 3, 7, and 5 to represent Arabic sounds. It’s widely used in chats, text messages, and social media.
